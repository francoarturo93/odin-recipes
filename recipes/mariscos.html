Rice With Seafood

Description

It is one of the most representative dishes of Peruvian marine gastronomy, born from the meeting of the most influential cuisines of the Creole recipe book. A combination of seafood, the intense flavor of the chili-based dressing, and rice, which embraces and welcomes everything.

Ingredients

  • 100 grams of prawns
  • 1 dozen shells
  • 1/2 dozen clams
  • 1/2 dozen choros (mussels)
  • 1/4 kg of squid
  • 1/4 kg of octopus
  • 1 kg of white rice
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro (cilantro)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 cup of peas (peas)
  • 3 tablespoons of panca pepper
  • 3 tablespoons of ground yellow chili
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 lemons
  • Parsley
  • choros (mussels) or fish broth, salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. First prepare the white rice that you like the most.
  2. While the rice is being prepared, put the prawns and the shells in boiling water, strain them and reserve them. It also prepares a broth with mussels and clams.
  3. Put enough water in a saucepan to cover the mussels and clams, add salt, pepper and let it cook until it reduces to more or less 1 cup of water and reserve.
  4. Now heat a frying pan with a little oil, add the chopped onion, the garlic, the peas and finally 1/2 cup of choros (mussels) broth, fry everything for a few minutes.
  5. When the peas are done, add the panca chili pepper, the yellow chili pepper, the tomato paste and stir well.
  6. Then add the squid, clams, and octopus and cook everything for a few minutes. Then add half of the chopped coriander.
  7. When the rice is ready, add it to the seafood preparation and add the mussels, the prawns, the shells that you prepared at the beginning and mix everything very well. Correct the salt and pepper.
